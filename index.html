About Runner

Runner App is a lightweight fitness companion that lets you explore a list of passionate runners and the company founders who guide them. Each runner has their own profile with personal stats like age, blood type, running experience, 5km personal record, number of races completed, and reviews that highlight their journey. You can view details about each runner, get inspired by their progress, and learn about the experts behind the fitness company. The app is designed to be simple, interactive, and motivating for anyone interested in running and fitness.
